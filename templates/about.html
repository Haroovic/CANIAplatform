How CANIA Works

Advanced AI technology meets compassionate care

DenseNet-121 Neural Architecture

CANIA is powered by a custom-trained deep learning model based on the DenseNet-121 architecture, specifically optimized for medical imaging analysis. Our AI has been trained on thousands of chest X-ray images to recognize subtle patterns that may indicate lung cancer.

The model processes uploaded X-ray images in seconds, analyzing tissue density, structural anomalies, and pattern recognition markers that correlate with malignant formations.

Results are delivered instantly with clear risk classifications, empowering both patients and healthcare providers with actionable insights.

2. AI Analysis
