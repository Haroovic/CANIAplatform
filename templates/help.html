Getting Started with CANIA

1

Create Your Account

Click "Join CANIA" in the navigation bar. Choose your role (Patient or Expert), fill in your details, and create a secure password. You'll need a valid email address.

2

Verify & Login

After registration, you can immediately log in with your email and password. Make sure to remember your credentials or use the "Remember Me" option.

3

Explore Your Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see options based on your role. Patients can access the Scanner, CANIA Space, and My Journey. Experts can access the Expert Dashboard.

Important: Choose the correct role during registration. Patients analyze scans and ask questions. Experts answer questions and add medical knowledge to the database.
4

Complete Your Profile

While optional, adding more information to your profile helps us provide better personalized recommendations and connect you with the right resources.

5

Start Using CANIA

You're all set! Patients can upload their first X-ray or explore CANIA Space. Experts can start answering questions or adding Q&As to help patients.

Guide for Patients

Using the AI Scanner

Step 1: Navigate to Scanner

From your dashboard or main menu, click on "Scanner" or "Get Started" button.

Step 2: Upload Your X-Ray

Click the upload area and select a chest X-ray image (JPG or PNG format). The image will appear in the upload box as a preview.

Step 3: Execute Scan

Click "Execute Risk Detection Scan". The AI will analyze your image in seconds.

Step 4: Review Results

You'll receive either a "High Risk" or "Low Risk" classification. HIGH RISK means patterns were detected - consult a radiologist immediately. LOW RISK means no concerning patterns were found, but continue regular check-ups.

Medical Disclaimer: CANIA provides preliminary screening only. Always consult with qualified medical professionals for diagnosis and treatment decisions.

Using CANIA Space

Search for Answers

Type keywords like "diet", "exercise", "sleep", "pain" into the search bar. CANIA will show you expert-verified Q&As matching your search.

Ask Your Own Question

Can't find what you're looking for? Scroll down and submit your own question. Medical experts will be notified and will answer within 24-48 hours.

Get Notified

When an expert answers your question, you'll receive a notification (bell icon in the header). Click it to see your answer.

View Full Answers

Click "Read Full Answer" on any search result to see the complete expert response and additional details.

Tracking Your Journey

Click "My Journey" to see a timeline of all your scans and results. Each entry shows the date, scan image, and AI prediction. This helps you and your doctor track changes over time.

Guide for Medical Experts

Adding New Q&As

1. Go to your Expert Dashboard
2. Fill in the "Add New Q&A" form:
   • Question: Write the question patients might ask
   • Keywords: Add searchable terms (comma-separated) like "diet, food, nutrition"
   • Answer: Provide detailed, accurate medical guidance
3. Click "Add Q&A" - it's immediately searchable by patients!

Answering Patient Questions

1. Check "Unanswered Patient Questions" section
2. Click "Answer" on any question
3. Add keywords so future patients can find this answer
4. Write your expert answer
5. Submit - the patient gets notified automatically!

Best Practices

• Be Clear: Use simple language patients can understand
• Be Complete: Provide thorough answers covering different aspects
• Be Accurate: Only provide information you're qualified to give
• Add Good Keywords: Think like a patient - what would they search?
• Stay Professional: Compassionate but medically sound advice

Note: As an expert, you have access to the Expert Dashboard and Q&A system only. You cannot access patient scans, journeys, or personal medical records to protect privacy.
